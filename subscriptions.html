Frequently Asked Questions

What are the different models and what are their benefits? There are three public models created by us at Faerie, which are Erik 1.0, Erik 2.0, and Pixie. Erik 1.0 is available to everyone, while to use Erik 2.0 and Pixie, you need to have a paid subscription. Erik 1.0 is a basic chatbot which can talk and has some basic thinking capabilities and also some other things. Erik 2.0 has over double the training of Erik 1.0 and can talk, think, and process data among other perks. Pixie is our offline model that requires around 100MB of storage. It is a bit less powerful than Erik 1.0, but can be used without WiFi. If you want a specific model, then you can create one! Free users can create 2 models per month, and you can upload your own training to create a personalized chatbot.
What are the experimental features? There are many features that we are playing around with, such as coding in multiple languages, image generation, flowcharting, and others. For a full list, click here. Until they get released, all experimental features / features in "beta" testing will be exclusively available to paid users.
